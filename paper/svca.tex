We present a compact artifact structure for reproducibility-first research workflows.

\\section{Artifact Model}
The artifact is composed of deterministic source compilation, manifest hashing, and signature verification.

Future work will report measured reproducibility metrics across environments.

\\section{Conclusion}
SVCA provides a minimal baseline for artifact-oriented science.
